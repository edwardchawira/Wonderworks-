One week, five activities

WonderWorks Holiday Club is a summer holiday club built on the belief that faith and creativity belong together. Psalm 78:4 puts it plainly:

"We will tell the next generation the praiseworthy deeds of the Lord, his power, and the wonders he has done."

That's the heart of this holiday club, passing faith on to the next generation through music, art and the everyday wonder of trying something new.

WonderWorks Holiday Club

oUR Mission

Your child will spend five days at WonderWorks Holiday Club, discovering God's wonders and developing their gifts.

Mornings start with worship and Bible teaching, then the day opens out into activities, keyboard or guitar, arts and crafts, movement, coding, singing, not just childcare to fill the hours.We operate at Pinnacle Sound Studios in Derby, with only eight children in the group, so your child is known by name, not lost in a crowd. By Friday, they've learned something real, made something with their own hands, and maybe found a gift they didn't know they had.

Pick your week

Week 1: Firm foundations

Monday 10th Aug - Friday 14th Aug

Children begin by learning the building blocks of music, creativity and faith. Through Bible stories, first keyboard or guitar lessons, arts and crafts, coding and worship, they'll discover what it means to build a life on a strong foundation that lasts.

"Everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man who built his house on the rock."Matthew 7:24

Week 2: Better Together

Monday 17th Aug - Friday 21st Aug

This week is all about learning together. As children sing, play music, create art and solve coding challenges as a team, they'll discover that every person has unique gifts and that God designed us to encourage and support one another.

"Now you are the body of Christ, and each one of you is a part of it."1 Corinthians 12:27

Week 3: Shine Your Light

Monday 24th Aug - friday 28th Aug

As children grow in confidence through music, singing, coding, arts and crafts, and time in God's Word, they'll be encouraged to recognise the gifts God has given them and use them to make a positive difference. This week is about discovering that every child has a light to shine and learning to use their talents with courage, kindness and faith.

"Let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven."Matthew 5:16
